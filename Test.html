About Golden Island

Golden Island is a green tourism project that combines music and culture, aiming to be an ideal destination for people around the world to explore, enjoy, and experience. Our mission is to create a tropical oasis in the heart of the city, offering a community where happiness is shared and every experience is cherished.

The Project

Golden Island focuses on sustainable tourism, blending traditional Vietnamese flavors with international culinary styles using local ingredients. Our project includes various activities such as water games, art spaces, and unique entertainment options, creating a vibrant and environmentally friendly space.

Explore Golden Island

Discover the beauty and excitement of Golden Island. From stunning landscapes to thrilling events, there is something for everyone to enjoy. Join us and create unforgettable memories at our outdoor music events, relaxing spas, and adventurous water games.
