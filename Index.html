Welcome to Golden Island

Golden Island is the ideal destination for unique and exciting adventures and a complete, delightful resort life in a tropical "oasis" right in the heart of the city.

"Golden Dreams at the Edge of the World" - Giấc mơ vàng nơi tận cùng thế giới.

Golden Dreams (Giấc Mơ Vàng)

The phrase "Golden Dreams" not only refers to the brand name "Golden Island" but also evokes images of high aspirations and dreams. It is an invitation for customers to come to Golden Island to realize their dreams and experiences. Whether it's relaxing, exploring unique cuisine, or enjoying special entertainment events.

At the Edge of the World (Tại Rìa Thế Giới)

This phrase carries symbolic meaning, indicating that Golden Island is an ideal "escape" from the busy and bustling reality, like a hidden paradise at the edge of the modern world. This relates to Golden Island's vision of creating a "tropical oasis" in the heart of the city, where customers can find relaxation and positive energy rejuvenation.
